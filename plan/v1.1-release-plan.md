**Status:** backlog (not started)

## Why this cycle exists

Per `plan/COMPETITIVE-ANALYSIS.md`, after 1.0.0 closes the table-stakes gaps, these 5 are the highest-leverage adds: features competitors charge for as paid add-ons or cite as differentiators in their marketing.

## Build order

1. **Sprint 1** — Referral system (#6) + Slack/Discord templates (#8) in parallel; both small
2. **Sprint 2** — Campaign multipliers (#7) + Web push (#9); decoupled
3. **Sprint 3** — Visual rule builder (#10) — biggest project, ~1-2 months

## Out of scope for 1.1.0 (deferred further)

Long-tail items from the analysis doc — CSV import/export, real-time leaderboard, Zapier listing, pay-for-points, avatar frames, multi-tier referral, anti-abuse beyond rate limits, explicit WPML/Polylang, GDPR hooks, mobile SDK. Re-evaluate after 1.1 ships.

## Why these 5 (and not others)

The competitive analysis ranked all 20 candidate adds. These 5 are the ones that:
- Show up in head-to-head comparison charts (push, Slack, multipliers)
- Drive viral growth (referrals)
- Lower the configuration bar (visual rule builder — currently our biggest UX gap)
